About Us

Ajeenkya D Y Patil School of MCA(DYPSOM) is one of the most prestigious academic groups in the Pune City and state of Maharashtra which is Approved by AICTE and Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. DYPSOM was established in 2015 by the Chairman of Ajeenkya D.Y.Patil Group.
